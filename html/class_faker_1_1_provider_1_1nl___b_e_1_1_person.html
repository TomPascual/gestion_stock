static Faker\Provider\nl_BE\Person::rrn ( $gender = null)
static

Belgian Rijksregister numbers are used to identify each citizen, it consists of three parts, the person's day of birth, in the format 'ymd', followed by a number between 1 and 997, odd for males, even for females. The last part is used to check if it's a valid number.
