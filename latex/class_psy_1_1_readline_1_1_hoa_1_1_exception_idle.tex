\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
{\ttfamily Hoa\textbackslash{}Exception\textbackslash{}Idle} is the mother exception class of libraries. The only difference between {\ttfamily Hoa\textbackslash{}Exception\textbackslash{}Idle} and its direct children {\ttfamily Hoa\textbackslash{}Exception} is that the latter fires events after beeing constructed.
